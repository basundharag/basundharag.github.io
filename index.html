Hi there, I'm Basundhara Ghosh!

I am a theoretical physicist working at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. I currently hold the DST-INSPIRE Faculty fellowship (granted by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India), in the group of Dr. Rajeev Kumar Jain. I specialise in the area of Cosmology, which attempts to understand the evolution of the Universe and how structures in the Universe came into being. I finished my PhD in 2020 from the University of Geneva under the supervision of Prof. Ruth Durrer where we focussed on galaxy number counts and relativistic effects therein.
